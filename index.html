My Baking Dessert Recipes

Author: z5632377,Jian Le

Chocolate Cake

  • 200g flour
  • 50g cocoa powder
  • 150g sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 150ml milk
  • 100g butter
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (356°F).
  2. Mix all dry ingredients together.
  3. Add eggs, milk, and melted butter. Stir until smooth.
  4. Pour into a baking tray and bake for 30 minutes.

Strawberry Milkshake

  • 100g strawberries
  • 200ml milk
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Ice cubes (optional)
  1. Put all ingredients into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and enjoy!

Crème Brûlée

  • 4 egg yolks
  • 50g sugar
  • 1 vanilla pod
  • 400ml heavy cream
  1. Heat the cream with the vanilla pod.
  2. Mix egg yolks with sugar, then slowly add the cream.
  3. Pour into ramekins and bake in a water bath at 160°C (320°F) for 30 minutes.
  4. Chill, then sprinkle sugar on top and caramelize with a torch.

Mango Pudding

  • 200g mango purée
  • 100ml milk
  • 100ml cream
  • 5g gelatin powder
  • 30g sugar
  1. Dissolve gelatin in warm water.
  2. Mix all ingredients and heat gently (do not boil).
  3. Pour into molds and refrigerate until set.

Donuts

  • 250g bread flour
  • 40g sugar
  • 3g yeast
  • 1 egg
  • 100ml milk
  • 30g butter
  1. Knead all ingredients into a smooth dough and let it rise for 1 hour.
  2. Roll out the dough and cut donut shapes. Let rise again for 30 minutes.
  3. Fry in hot oil until golden brown and drain.
  4. Decorate with powdered sugar or chocolate glaze.
